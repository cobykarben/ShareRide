# ShareRide Website Development Plan

## Project Overview
*Add your project description and goals here*

the point of this project is to create a software serivice for users to list and find car rides to nearby events. this differs from uber and other rideshare apps in the sense that the people who list rides are already going to that specific event and have extra room in their car to bring whoever signs up. there are two kinds of app users: drivers and riders. drivers can post rides, detailing the type of car they drive, and any already taken seats in the ride. then, users will be able to look up the event (for example: YU vs NYU soccer game; Anne and Zev's wedding; US Open) and see which cars driven by who are going there. then, if they click on a listed ride, they will have a seating map equivelant to those found on airline companies where you have a "cabin" view of the car and you can select your desired the seat. the cabin view will mimic the style of car, like mini van, sedan, pickup truck, or van layout so riders get an understanding of where they are sitting and even who they are sitting next to if others already signed up. Riders can then choose to apply for the ride, and the driver will view their profile and accept. riders and drivers will have to have profile pictures, and be able to friend request fellow uesers to make the approval process easier.

There will also be a feature where the driver when he lists the ride in the first place can include the expected costs of the trip like tolls and gasoline, that can be split amongst all the users either with or without the driver having to pay (driver's choice) when making listing. then, riders will see this information on the listing, and make the commitmeent to pay that when they get approved for the ride. after the ride, the driver has the ability to adjust the costs, which will only needed to be paid and split by those who approve of the added cost.
